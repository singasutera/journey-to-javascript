Grading System Project

This is a Grading System project. We will give you a grade from A to F based on your score.

The rules are as follows:

  • If your score is 80 or above, you will get an A.
  • If your score is 70 or above, you will get a B.
  • If your score is 60 or above, you will get a C.
  • If your score is 50 or above, you will get a D.
  • If your score is 40 or above, you will get an E.
  • If your score is below 40, you will get an F.
